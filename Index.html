About Us

Trusted Manufacturer & Supplier of Industrial Instrumentation, Precision Engineering Components in India

Mars Instrumentation Industries is a leading industrial instrumentation manufacturer in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, specializing in the design, manufacturing, and supply of high-precision engineering products for industrial, surveying, and infrastructure applications.

With a strong focus on quality, accuracy, and durability, we manufacture a wide range of industrial fittings, control valves, mechanical couplings, and surveying instruments that comply with national and international quality standards.

Our advanced manufacturing facility, skilled workforce, and strict quality control processes enable us to serve clients across oil & gas, construction, infrastructure, manufacturing, and engineering sectors. We are committed to delivering reliable, cost-effective, and customized solutions that meet our customers’ exact requirements.
